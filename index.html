On Humanity
We are NOT ManKind, for we have proven time and time again that we are not inherently kind. Tell me what that has gotten us? We are NOT the Human Race, for we are one and thus that implies we are no more than dogs chasing their own tails in a circle. Where has that gotten us? We are NOT Humanity, for that is the capability of Love, Compassion, Kindness and Understanding that we are Capable of. Humanity is what we STRIVE to BECOME...
— Michael Ellsworth
Look Upon This World
Look upon the past and know our grief, not that of my people or yours but all humans alike. Look upon the present and know our fears and insecurities, not those of my people or yours but of all those who live. It is time NOW to put ignorant judgement of others based on color, religion or what mass of land our blood hails from to rest. It is NOW that we MUST more than EVER before come together to push forward and progress. It is NOW that we MUST look to the FUTURE and see the pain, suffering, death and chaos that we have and are causing. To the planet and creatures that give us life and bring beauty to our world. To one another. I say toss borders and differences aside for it is time to unify. My strength lies not only in who I am alone nor what I can achieve alone. My strength lies in those who help me. As well I will offer my strength to those others who might need to call upon it. Always remember to keep smiling, be kind to ALL including yourself and ALWAYS work to forge a world where we can ALL live in peace, happiness and health together.
— Michael Ellsworth
